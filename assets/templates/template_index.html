I am the Data Science Lead at LVIS corporation, a Palo Alto based start-up building cutting-edge neural information analysis technology. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University, M.S. from University of California Los Angeles, and B.S. from Zhejiang University, China. My research interests are in the area of medical image and deep learning, with a special focus on MRI super-resolution, compressed-sensing reconstruction, EEG abnormality detection, and brain connectivity analysis.
